Twalky is my first two-motor walker. It uses a microcore brain and a 74HC245 motor driver. The walker is actually incomplete because of noise being induced by the motors into the circuit. I am still working on debugging it. The body is complete, the microcore has been free-formed, and I plan to make a PCB at some point.
