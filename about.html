I began exploring robotics in the early 2000s, at a time when finding components and tutorials in India was a challenge. Most of what I learned came from experimentation, online communities, and repurposed parts. This website originally lived on solarbotics.net, which eventually went offline. The site has now been rebuilt and modernized for 2025.

Why this website?

The main purpose of this site is to share practical BEAM robotics knowledge using locally available materials—something that was hard to find when I started. Over the years, many hobbyists reached out looking for guidance, and reviving this website felt like the right way to preserve those early learnings while keeping them accessible to anyone interested today.
